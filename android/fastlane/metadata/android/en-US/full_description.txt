Climber Eye is a simple Flutter application that allows you to compare two videos simultaneously. It's designed to help analyze and compare movements, techniques, or any other visual details in two videos.

Features:

*   Side-by-side video playback.
*   Independent playback controls for each video.
*   Adjustable playback speed.
*   Precise seeking.
*   Zoom and pan.

This app is open-source and released under the GPLv3 license.
